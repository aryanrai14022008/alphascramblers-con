Some General Questions....
Q: What is counselling and what can I expect?
A: Counseling, also known as therapy or psychotherapy, is a process where a trained professional (a counselor or therapist) helps individuals explore their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors to address personal challenges, improve mental well-being, and achieve personal growth. You can expect a safe, confidential, and non-judgmental space where you can openly discuss your concerns and work towards your goals.
