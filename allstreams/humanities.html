Arts/Humanities
Know Your Stream:

The Humanities stream in high school focuses on subjects related to human culture, society, and the human experience. It encompasses a wide range of disciplines, including history, literature, philosophy, languages, and the arts, with the goal of fostering critical thinking, communication skills, and cultural awareness.
